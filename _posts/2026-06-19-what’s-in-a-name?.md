Names are symbols whose meanings are created, interpreted and reshaped by human interaction and shared understanding.

Names as symbols
======
Names are not just labels, they symbolize identity, destiny or divine purpose.

Meanings emerge through context
======
The significance of a name is often shaped by the story around it. People interpret names differently depending on; cultural background, religious teachings, personal experiences etc.

Interaction shapes interpretation
======
Over time, communities reinterpret names through teaching, preaching or discussion. A name’s meaning can evolve as people interact with text and with each other.

Identity construction
======
In scriptures, names often signal transformation:
	Abram - Abraham
	Sarai - Sarah
	Saul - Paul


Moral
------
Not every English name is a Christian name and some non-English names have scriptural meanings.
